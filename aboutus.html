Project Motivation

The motivation behind this project came from the increasing need for sustainable energy solutions in today's world. By harnessing the potential of piezoelectric cells, we aim to demonstrate how mechanical energy (like footsteps or vehicles on roads) can be converted into electrical energy to power everyday infrastructure.

Our Approach

Our research involved a thorough investigation of piezoelectric materials, conducting experiments under various mechanical stress conditions to determine how much energy can be harvested and used efficiently in street lighting systems.
